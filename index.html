About

A brochure for Louisa Palmi's work Ashes to Ashes, a composition that portrays the transition from life to death by transforming the body's decomposition processes into sound. Exhibited at The Cell, 2024.

Book layout and cover design for Ordkonst's issue Jubileum, celebrating 30 years as a non-profit organization, 2024.

Hermes 2000 is a website based on Sweden's very first newspaper, Hermes Gothicus, from 1625. Using my own transcriptions and translations with the help of ChatGPT, the website features 400-year-old news, which can be read in both modern Swedish and the older "Early New Swedish." As part of the course Graphic Identity, led by Laslo Strong, we were tasked with reviving outdated companies by creating a new visual identity for them.

Exhibition poster for the course Graphic Identityat Beckmans College of Design, 2024.

Sublime exposes viewers to fleeting images in contrast to constant imagery, questioning the boundary between what we see and what we actually absorb, and how these impressions affect our subconscious. From the course Art, Advertising, Art at Beckmans College of Design, 2024.

Luftslott is a magazine focusing on the emerging generation of creators. Through an Open Call, creators of all kinds can submit contributions to the magazine. The aim of Luftslott is to provide a unique platform for new, emerging art and culture. Curation and art direction by Agnes Nebrelius. Design by Adam Arinbjarnarson, Molly Schedin, and Phoebe Crookes. 2023.

Book layout and cover design for Ordkonst's issue Ikon, 2023.

Landscapes, 2024. An animated music video made during the course Motion Graphics and Image led by Jonas Johansson at Beckmans College of Design.
